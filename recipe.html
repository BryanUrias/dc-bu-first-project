Tomatoes and Ham

This is considered a poor man's recipe. It consists of using left over processed ham and Tomatoes in order to make a delicious, somewhat hot, tomato/meat slush.

These are the ingredients you need

  • tomatoes
  • ham
  • garlic powder
  • oil of choice

How to prepare meal (5 minute prep / 5 minute cook time)

  1. Cut the ham
  2. cut the tomato into small pieces
  3. Heat up skillet (medium heat)
  4. oil skillet
  5. toss all ingredients on skillet
  6. add garlic powder
